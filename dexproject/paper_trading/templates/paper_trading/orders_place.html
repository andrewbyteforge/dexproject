Order Types Explained
Limit Buy:

Buy when price drops to or below your target price.

Limit Sell:

Sell when price rises to or above your target price.

Stop Limit Buy:

Buy after price breaks above trigger, up to limit price.

Stop Limit Sell:

Trailing Stop:

Dynamic stop loss that follows price upward, locks in profits.
